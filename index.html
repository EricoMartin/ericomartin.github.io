    About

    The vision for this application was birthed through first-hand experience of how clients and drivers alike suffer terrible experiences from costly fares, harassment by security agents, meager profit made by drivers, vehicle theft and loss of personal belongings, the list is endless. The E-Hailer Rider App brings you a seamless experience where you can enjoy the best benefits while being rest assured of top-quality service.
